Personal Experience Guidelines

Data Sources

  • First-hand Knowledge: Utilize your direct experiences, such as cultural practices, traditions, or encounters you’ve personally participated in.
  • Local Online Forums: Gather insights from community discussions on platforms like Reddit, local Q&A sites, social media groups, or regional forums.
  • Community Observations: Reflect on interactions or practices you’ve observed within your community, workplace, or social environments.
  • Cultural Events: Draw from festivals, ceremonies, or traditions you’ve attended or have a deep understanding of.

Quality Standards

  • Cultural Authenticity: Ensure scenarios reflect genuine cultural practices, not stereotypes
  • Local Specificity: Include details that only locals would know (specific locations, customs, terminology)
  • Contextual Richness: Provide enough background for the scenario to feel realistic and complete
  • Clear Objectives: Make questions unambiguous with definitive, culturally-informed answers
  • Appropriate Difficulty: Balance accessibility with cultural knowledge requirements

Making It Challenging for AI

Create questions that require deep cultural understanding:

  • Long-Tail Entities: Use specific local places, brands, or terms instead of generic ones
  • Implicit Knowledge: Reference cultural norms that aren't explicitly stated but are understood locally
  • Conditional Reasoning: Create scenarios requiring multi-step cultural logic
  • Temporal Context: Include time-sensitive cultural references (seasons, holidays, historical periods)
  • Social Nuances: Incorporate subtle social dynamics, hierarchies, or etiquette

Avoid These Pitfalls

  • Generic Scenarios: Avoid situations that could apply to any culture
  • Obvious Questions: Don't ask about widely known facts or stereotypes
  • Ambiguous Answers: Ensure there's one clearly correct cultural response
  • Outdated References: Use current cultural practices, not historical ones
  • Personal Opinions: Focus on cultural facts, not subjective preferences
